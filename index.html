Common Questions

Things people usually want to know.

Don't see your question? Text (984) 344‑6217 and I'll get back to you.

Do I need to be home for the service?

Nope. As long as I have access to the area being cleaned and an outdoor water spigot, you don't need to be there. I'll text when I'm on the way and again when I'm done so you can check the work.

When and how do I pay?

After the job is done. Walk the work with me, make sure it looks right, then pay. Cash or CashApp — no card required, no deposit needed to book.

What if I'm not happy with the results?

I come back and re-do it — free. No questions, no invoice. Satisfaction isn't optional. If a spot still isn't right, I'd rather fix it than have you pay for something you don't love.

What if it rains on my appointment?

I'll text you the morning of to reschedule. Light drizzle is fine — actual rain means we move it. No cancellation fee, we just find a new slot that week.

Will the soap or bleach hurt my grass or pets?

No. I use a pet-safe, eco-friendly soap for all services. The bleach treatment in Pristine+ is diluted and applied carefully so it doesn't run into your lawn or landscaping. A quick rinse of surrounding plants before I start is standard.

Do you bring your own equipment and water?

I bring all the equipment — pressure washer, hoses, soap, nozzles, the works. I do need access to your outdoor water spigot (or hose bib) to supply the wash. That's the only thing I need from you.

Do you only work in Highcroft?

Highcroft and the neighborhoods right around it (Cary, Morrisville, Preston, Amberly, and nearby). If you're a bit farther out, text and ask — I'm often happy to make the trip for bigger jobs.

How far in advance should I book?

Booking opens Thursday, June 11, 2026 and runs through August. Same-week availability is usually easy, but weekends fill up faster — book early if you want a specific day.
